How Operation Lumina Works

1. Your Child Receives Their Field Kit

A beautifully packaged kit arrives with 30 premium mission cards, a 3D star vessel, and their Founding Agent credentials.

2. Download the Companion App

Set up your parent portal and create your child's agent profile. The app syncs with the physical cards via QR codes.

3. Complete Missions Together

Your child chooses a mission, listens to character guidance, and completes it in the real world. You scan the QR code to verify and celebrate!

4. Watch Their Star Fill with Light

Each mission adds light to their star vessel (10 points). At 100 points, they receive a personal message from Aurora Frost!
