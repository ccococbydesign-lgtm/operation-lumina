# Audio Recording Scripts for Operation Lumina

These scripts will help you create the voice-over recordings for your website. Record these with a warm, friendly female voice to embody Aurora's character.

## üéôÔ∏è Recording Tips

**Voice Direction:**
- Warm and nurturing, like a caring older sister or young aunt
- Clear articulation but conversational
- Gentle pacing - not rushed
- Smile while recording (it comes through in the voice!)
- Add subtle emotion - excitement, wonder, care

**Technical Setup:**
- Use a quiet room with soft furnishings (reduces echo)
- USB microphone recommended (Blue Yeti, Audio-Technica, etc.)
- Record as WAV first, then convert to MP3
- Aim for -12dB to -6dB levels (not too quiet, not clipping)
- Remove background noise in Audacity (free software)

**Optional Enhancements:**
- Add gentle background music (royalty-free from Epidemic Sound or Artlist)
- Keep music volume at 20-30% of voice volume
- Use nature sounds (wind chimes, soft bells) as subtle accents
- Add a tiny bit of reverb for warmth (but don't overdo it!)

## üé¨ Production Workflow

### Step 1: Record Raw Audio
1. Find a quiet room
2. Use a good microphone
3. Record each script 2-3 times (pick the best take)
4. Save as high-quality WAV files

### Step 2: Edit (Use Audacity - Free)
1. Import your recording
2. Remove silence at beginning/end
3. Apply "Noise Reduction" effect
4. Normalize volume to -12dB
5. Add gentle fade in/out (1-2 seconds)

### Step 3: Add Music (Optional)
1. Import background music track
2. Place it underneath your voice
3. Reduce music volume to 20-30% of voice
4. Fade music in before you speak
5. Fade out after you finish

### Step 4: Export
1. Export as MP3
2. Settings: 128kbps or 192kbps
3. Keep file under 5MB
4. Name exactly as specified in this guide
